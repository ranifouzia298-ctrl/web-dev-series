"Pasta Recipe At Home"

Prepa Cooke Serve
30 mins 20 mins 10 mins

Ingrediants

  • olive oil
  • Butter
  • Fresh garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • pepper

Method

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until tender.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil/butter for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Combine cooked pasta with the garlic oil, adding a splash of pasta water.
  4. Toss with Parmesan and seasonings, then serve
